This page provides information about basic dog care

Dog Park Information

Here are some dog parks in the area:
  • Central Dog Park: Open daily from 6 AM to 9 PM.
  • Northside Park: Features a large off-leash area and agility equipment.

Foods Dogs Shouldn't Eat

It's important to keep your dog safe by avoiding these harmful foods:
  • Chocolate: Contains theobromine, which is toxic to dogs.
  • Grapes and raisins: Can cause kidney failure in dogs.
  • Onions and garlic: Can lead to anemia in dogs.

Health Tips

Regular Vet Visits:
  • Ensure your dog gets regular check-ups to monitor their health.
  • Keep vaccinations up to date.
Exercise:
  • Provide daily exercise appropriate for your dog’s breed and age.
  • Include both physical and mental stimulation.

Nutrition

Balanced Diet:
  • Offer a balanced diet tailored to your dog’s age, size, and health needs.
  • Avoid feeding table scraps and ensure their food is nutritionally complete.
Hydration:
  • Always provide fresh, clean water.

Training and Socialization

Basic Training:
  • Teach basic commands like sit, stay, and come.
  • Use positive reinforcement techniques.
Socialization:
  • Introduce your dog to different environments, people, and other animals.

Grooming

Regular Grooming:
  • Brush your dog regularly to maintain a healthy coat.
  • Bathe as needed and trim nails.
Dental Care:
  • Brush your dog's teeth regularly.
  • Provide dental chews or toys.

Safety

Home Safety:
  • Dog-proof your home by removing hazards.
  • Provide a safe space for your dog to relax.
Travel Safety:
  • Use appropriate restraints or carriers when traveling by car.
  • Ensure your dog is microchipped and has an ID tag.
